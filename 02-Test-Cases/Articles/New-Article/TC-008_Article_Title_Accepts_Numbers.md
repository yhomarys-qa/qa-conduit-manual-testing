# TC-008 - Article Title Accepts Numbers

## Steps
1. Open the Conduit application
2. Log in with valid credentials
3. Navigate to "New Article"
4.  Enter a title using numbers (e.g., "123456")
5.  Fill all other required fields with valid data
6. Click on "Publish Article"

## Expected Result
The system should accept the title and the article should be successfully created
