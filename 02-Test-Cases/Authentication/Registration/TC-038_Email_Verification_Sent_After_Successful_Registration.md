# TC-038 - Redirect to “Finalize Registration” After Successful Signup


## Steps
1. Open the Conduit application
2. Navigate to the Sign up page
3. Enter valid registration data (Username, Email, Password, Confirm Password)
4. Click on the "Sign up" button
5. Access the inbox of the registered email address

## Expected Result
A verification email should be sent to the registered email address after successful registration.
The email should contain a verification code or verification link required to complete the registration process.
