# 🐞 BUG-13 - Incorrect Placeholder in “Email or Username” Field

## 📌 Summary
The login field labeled "Email or Username" displays an incorrect placeholder, showing only "Email" instead of indicating that both username and email are accepted.

---

## 🧪 Preconditions
- Login page is accessible in the browser  

---

## 🔁 Steps to Reproduce
1. Go to the login page  
2. Observe the "Email or Username" input field  

---

## ❌ Actual Result
The field displays the placeholder "Email" only, which does not reflect the actual expected input format.

---

## ✅ Expected Result
The field should display the placeholder:
> "Enter your username or email"

---

## 📊 Severity
Low

---

## 📎 Environment
- Windows 10  
- Chrome 145.0.7632.76 (64-bit)  
- App version: Demo  

---
