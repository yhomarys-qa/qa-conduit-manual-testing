# 🐞 BUG-19 - “Forgot Your Password?” Link Not Displayed on Login Page

## 📌 Summary
The login page does not display the "Forgot your password?" link, preventing users from accessing the password recovery flow.

---

## 🧪 Preconditions
- Login page is accessible in the browser  
- User is registered in the system with valid credentials  

---

## 🔁 Steps to Reproduce
1. Access the login page  
2. Look for the "Forgot your password?" link  
3. Attempt to locate or click the link  

---

## ❌ Actual Result
The "Forgot your password?" link is not displayed on the login page.

---

## ✅ Expected Result
The login page should display the "Forgot your password?" link, which should redirect the user to the password recovery page when clicked.

---

## 📊 Severity
High

---

## 📎 Environment
- Windows 10  
- Chrome 145.0.7632.76 (64-bit)  
- App version: Demo  

---
